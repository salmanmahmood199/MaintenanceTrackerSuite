Putting It All Together
Each article’s HTML structure should look roughly like this:

Edit
<article class="page-container">
  <h1 class="article-title">[Article Title]</h1>
  <p class="article-meta">[Date] · by [Author]</p>

  <h2 class="section-heading">Introduction</h2>
  <p>…introductory text…</p>

  <h2 class="section-heading">Key Techniques in ML‑Driven Maintenance</h2>
  <h3 class="subsection-heading">1. Time‑Series Analysis & Forecasting</h3>
  <p>…details…</p>
  <!-- more subsections -->

  <div class="case-study">
    <h4>Case Study: Automotive Manufacturing</h4>
    <p>…case details…</p>
  </div>

  <h2 class="conclusion">Conclusion</h2>
  <p>…summary…</p>

  <h2 class="section-heading">References</h2>
  <ol class="references">
    <li>[1] McKinsey & Company. (2024). The state of AI in 2023…</li>
    <!-- more references -->
  </ol>
</article>
